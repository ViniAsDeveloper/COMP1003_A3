The game consists of a minefield, represented by a grid with several squares arranged in rows and columns.
Each square may or may not contain a mine, and your aim is to reveal all safe squares (those that don't contain a mine).
However, if you click on a square that contains a mine, it will explode and you’ll lose the game!

To select a square, simply type the X and Y coordinates of the square. Then, you can choose an action to perform over this square.
Your available actions are:

1. Inspect -> revelas if there is a bomb on this square or not; You must do this for every safe square in the game")
2. Flag -> Marks the square as a bomb. It makes easier to keep track of all places you are sure to have a bomb
3. Question -> Marks the square with a question mark (?), meaning 'it is possibly a bomb' to warn you to take care
4. Help -> Displays this rules

That is it, super simple! Have fun!
