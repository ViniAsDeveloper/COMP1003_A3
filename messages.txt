rules
//$@#//
The game consists of a minefield, represented by a grid with several squares
arranged in rows and columns.

Each square may or may not contain a mine, and your goal is to reveal all
safe squares (those that don't contain a mine).

However, if you choose a square that contains a mine, it will explode
and youвҖҷll lose the game!

To play, you must choose an action to perform.
Your available actions are:

1. Inspect -> Reveals if there is a bomb on the square or not; You must
do this for every safe square in the game. If there is a bomb, you explode
and loose the game! If not, this will tell you how many bombs are around
this square.

2. Flag -> Marks the square as a bomb (F). It makes easier to keep track
of all places you are sure to have a bomb.

3. Question -> Marks the square with a question mark (?), meaning it is
possibly a bomb, to warn you to take care.

4. Help -> Displays this rules.

5. Save and quit -> Saves the current session to be played later and exits.

6. Quit -> Exits without saving the current session.
